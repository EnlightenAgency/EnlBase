About: Damian Strong

Currently a Software Engineer at Enlighten

History

My Passion for programming began in highschool where I worked as a Software Developer for the Allen Park robotics team 815 Advanced Power.
After highschool I decided to grow my passion at Unviersity of Michigan-Dearborn where I worked towards a degree in computer information science with a specialization in video game design
During my senior year at U of M as part of my senior design class I met Sandy Kronenberg and Paul Proctor who pitched an idea for a gps tracking application.
There were originally twelve developers working on the project and after the year was up I was one of three developers who was hired on full time to start Locqus LLC
I worked as the primary web developer for Locqus for three years doing everything from designing, building, deploying, and maintaining the Field Service Management web application
I was also lead in most of our third party integrations which included;

  • Google Maps
  • MapQuest
  • Weather Underground
  • Moneris
  • Vantiv
  • TomTom
  • QuickBooks
  • Threat Metrix
  • Transunion
